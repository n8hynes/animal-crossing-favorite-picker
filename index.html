Favorite Animal Crossing Villager Picker

There are so many animal crossing villagers, how can you possibly pick your favorites without vigorous testing? Use this tool to find your personal favorites!

Click here to show/hide settings.

Settings

The minimum number of characters shown at a time. If there are fewer characters than the minimum left in the round, you will see fewer than the minimum.

The maximum number of characters shown at a time. When there are few characters left in a round, you'll see fewer than the maximum, but greater than the minimum. Reducing the maximum batch size may make it easier to pick, but it will also take longer to get through each round. If the minimum batch size is greater than the maximum, the minimum number will be shown.

Mode: Display only regular villagers, only special characters, or both. Other filters do not apply to special characters.

Games to include: , , , , , , , Characters that have appeared in at least one of the selected games will appear. * Note that the New Horizons list will be incomplete until release.

Some characters never made it out of Japan. De-select this box to hide those characters.

Manual reset: Resets all your progress. Careful!

Background color: , , , , , , , , Select the color of the background. Select 'none' for a plain white background.
